      Preparation

      Recommended Cooking Instructions
      - Cooking time may vary depending on equipment, always cook until internal temperature of 74C.
      - Not recommended: boiling in water or deep frying. Do not consume raw.

      For Dishes Like Tacos, Pasta Sauce, and Chilli:
      1. Heat non-stick pan over medium-high heat, add Beyond Mince.
      2. Cook for 5-8 minutes, breaking up to desired crumble size and stirring frequently.
      3. If adding sauce, simmer for up to 30 minutes.
